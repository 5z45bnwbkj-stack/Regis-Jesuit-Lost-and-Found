How It Works

A clean workflow for a messy school problem.

The system is designed around the exact process a real school needs: reports, staff approval, searchable inventory, student claims, and verified returns.

01

Report

A student or staff member submits a found item with details, location, date, category, and optional photo.

02

Approve

Staff review new reports before they appear publicly, preventing duplicates and unclear posts.

03

Claim

Students submit proof of ownership using unique details like initials, stickers, scratches, or contents.

04

Return

Staff mark the item ready for pickup or returned, creating a clear end-to-end recovery record.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim an item?

Open Browse Items, choose the item, and submit evidence that proves it belongs to you.

Why do staff approve reports first?

Approval prevents duplicates, unclear descriptions, and inaccurate posts from appearing publicly.

Where do I pick up approved items?

Approved items can be picked up during highlighted pickup periods shown on My Profile.

Staff status update
function updateItem(id, status) {
  const item = items.find(item => item.id === id);
  if (!item) return;
  item.status = status;
  saveData();
  renderAll();
}
